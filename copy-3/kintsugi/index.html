Bones Whisper

Bones Whisper is a fast flow focused platformer where you explore a hidden city of bones and discover the stories and woes of the inhabitants of this shrouded world. Movement and levels are fine tuned to allow for deep movement options. Bones whisper is a personal project I have been working on and off for many years.

Play Now On Itch
Engine GameMaker Studio 2
Role Solo Developer
Status Demo on Itch and GXC

Level Design

With the project's long development I had the ability to iterate over the level design more than any other project with the first level getting completely redesigned from the ground up along with a movement system redesign. With the long development I have been able to create over an hour of content with 35 minutes of complete polished content.

Movement System

The movement system in this game is a very polished system that I believe has a great balance of easy to learn mechanics with a lot of depth allowing crazy amounts of mastery enabling a high skill ceiling for speedrunners to get insane times while being accessible enough that a 8 year old was able to beat all the currently completed content.
