about game

Bones Whisper is a platformer that has a sense of mastery and is a framework for exploring the world, stories, and characters in a immortal world.

The game revolves around momentum-based movement with the main mechanics being bounces and dashes that can be combined together to have lots of movement options.

The the first level of the game and teaches the player attacking, breakable boxes, jumping, switches, gates, crumbling blocks, down attacks, lantern bounces, and dashing. The setting is an outdoor area with dry vegetation and a lot of gothic architecture and mysteries to uncover.
